How To Get Started

Step 1

The very first step is to acquire a storage device with enough memory to store your torrents. I recommend an external hard drive with at least 1TB worth of space. On average each movie is 800mb in size while an hour long TV show is 400mb in size. I personally have 2 external hard drives with a total size of 5TB.

Step 2

The second step is to find and download a torrent file manager. The top 2 are UTorrent and BitTorrent These 2 programs are the most widely used out of all the available file managers Figure out which torrent file manager interface you like and set up a file folder on your hard drive to store the torrents

Step 3

The next step is to find your favorite torrent websites. The top 3 sites that I use are EZTV Kickass and Pirate Bay. Each one of these sites focuses on a specific genre of video. For example I get most of my TV shows from EZTV while I get my movies from either Kickass or Pirate Bay.

Step 4

The following step is to make sure you have fast enough download speed from your internet provider. Due to the size of the files having a fast download speed allows for your torrents to complete in hours instead of days. If you want to be able to download multiple files at a single time you need to have at least 7mb of download speed. I personally utilize a 12mb speed DSL connection and it works wonderfully.

Step 5

The final step will be to enjoy the countless shows and movies that you can download and watch at any time.
