Lists

List elements are used to put a specific type of list onto your webpage. There are 3 different types, ordered list, unordered list, and description list. Ordered lists will list your data with either numbers or letters. Unordered lists will list your data using a disc. Description lists will list your data based on term and then definition.

Ordered List Example

  1. East
  2. West
  3. North
  4. South

Unordered List Example

  • East
  • West
  • South
  • North

Description List Example

East
Japan is called the nation of the rising sun, since the sun rises in the East
West
The wild West was a major part in the gold rush in the United States. Sun sets in the West
South
The south lost the cival war. Mexico is South of the United States
North
The great white North is also known as Canada. Canada is North of the United States
